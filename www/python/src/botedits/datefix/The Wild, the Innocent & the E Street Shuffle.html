87== Rejected songs ==87== Rejected songs ==
88On November 4 and 5, 1997, eight tracks not included on ''The Wild, the Innocent and the E Street Shuffle'' were mixed and evaluated for inclusion on the ''[[Tracks (Bruce Springsteen album)|Tracks]]'' box set, to be released in 1998. They included "[[Zero and Blind Terry]]", "Thundercrack", "Seaside Bar Song", "Santa Ana", "[[Bishop Danced]]", "Evacuation of the West" aka "No More Kings In Texas", "Phantoms" and "Fire on the Wing". The first five, which included a live version of "Bishop Danced", recorded on January 31, 1973, at Max's Kansas City, New York City, were included; "Phantoms", "Fire on the Wing" and "No More Kings/Evacuation" remain officially unreleased.<ref>{{cite book |last1=White |first1=Ryan |title=Bruce Springsteen Album by Album |date=2016 |publisher=Palazzo Editions Ltd. |location=London, UK |pages=174–179 |url=https://www.palazzoeditions.com/ |access-date=7 December 2019}}</ref>88On November 4 and 5, 1997, eight tracks not included on ''The Wild, the Innocent and the E Street Shuffle'' were mixed and evaluated for inclusion on the ''[[Tracks (Bruce Springsteen album)|Tracks]]'' box set, to be released in 1998.
