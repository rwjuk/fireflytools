478Principal industries are wood products, agriculture, mining, and manufacturing. Linn County's economy relies heavily on the lumber and wood products industry; in 1990, this industry accounted for 40% of the county's manufacturing jobs. The climate and soil conditions provide one of Oregon's most diversified agriculture areas, allowing a wide variety of specialty crops such as common and perennial ryegrass. Linn County is also home to the only [[Emery (mineral)|emery]] mine in the United States, and the production of manufactured and motor homes.478Principal industries are wood products, agriculture, mining, and manufacturing.
