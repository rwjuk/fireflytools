n118'''Zond 5''' ({{lang-ru|Зонд 5|lit=Probe 5}}) was a spacecraft of the Soviet [[Zond program]]. In {{Date|September 1968|}} it became the second spaceship to travel to and circle the [[Moon]], and the first to return safely to Earth. In September 1968 it became the second spaceship to travel to and circle the [[Moon]], and the first to return safely to Earth. Zond 5 carried the first terrestrial organisms to the vicinity of the Moon, including two tortoises, fruit fly eggs, and plants.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://blogs.discovermagazine.com/crux/2018/09/18/zond-5-soviet-tortises-around-the-moon/|title=The First Earthlings Around the Moon Were Two Soviet Tortoises|last=Betz|first=Eric|date=18 September 2018|work=[[Discover (magazine)|Discover]]|access-date=14 July 2019}}</ref> The tortoises underwent biological changes during the flight, but it was concluded that the changes were primarily due to starvation and that they were little affected by space travel.

n135{{Nowrap|Zond 5}} launched on {{Date|14 September 1968|}} at 9:42.10{{nbs}}UTC, from [[Baikonur Cosmodrome Site 81|Site 81]] at the [[Baikonur Cosmodrome]]. The thrust of the third-stage rocket was terminated at {{convert|160|km|mi}}, which was the start of a 251-second coast. {{Nowrap|[[Block D]]}}, the upper-stage rocket, ignited and burned for 108 seconds, placing the spacecraft into a parking orbit of {{convert|191|by(x)|219|km|mi}}. Fifty-six minutes into the parking orbit the {{Nowrap|Block D}} fired a final time for the [[trans-lunar injection]].<ref name=rsw /> After this maneuver, the launch was announced to the world.{{sfn|Siddiqi|2003|p=654}} Mission Control discovered a problem with {{Nowrap|Zond 5}}'s attitude and traced the cause to a contaminated star tracker. Heat caused some of the interior coating to [[Outgassing|outgas]],{{sfn|Siddiqi|2003|p=655}} which delayed an attitude correction on the way to the Moon. The maneuver was performed {{convert|325000|km|mi}} from Earth using the Sun and the Earth as reference points.<ref name=rsw />n135{{Nowrap|Zond 5}} launched on 14 September 1968 at 9:42.10{{nbs}}UTC, from [[Baikonur Cosmodrome Site 81|Site 81]] at the [[Baikonur Cosmodrome]].
