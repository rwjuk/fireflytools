104The [[economy of Sindh]] is the second-largest in Pakistan after the province of Punjab; its provincial capital of [[Karachi]] is the [[List of cities in Pakistan by population|most populous city in the country]] as well as its main financial hub. Sindh is home to a large portion of [[Industry of Pakistan|Pakistan's industrial sector]] and contains two of the country's busiest commercial seaports: [[Port Qasim]] and the [[Port of Karachi]].
