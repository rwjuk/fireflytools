34'''''Chill Out''''' is an album by [[reggae]] band [[Black Uhuru]], released in 1982 (see [[1982 in music]]). The album was recorded at [[Channel One Studios]] in [[Jamaica]] and [[record producer|produced]] by [[Sly and Robbie]].
