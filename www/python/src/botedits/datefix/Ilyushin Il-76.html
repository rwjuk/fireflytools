32===Origins===32===Origins===
n33The aircraft was first conceived by [[Ilyushin]] in 1967 to meet a requirement for a freighter able to carry a payload of {{convert|40|t|lb}} over a range of {{convert|5000|km|nmi mi}} in less than six hours, able to operate from short and unprepared airstrips, and capable of coping with the worst weather conditions likely to be experienced in Siberia and the Soviet Union's Arctic regions. It was intended to replace the [[Antonov An-12]]. Another intended version was a double-decked 250-passenger airliner but that project was cancelled.
