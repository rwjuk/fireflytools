375===Establishing a park===375===Establishing a park===
t376In the 1930s, many people who loved the Big Bend country saw that it was a land of unique contrast and beauty that was worth preserving for future generations. In 1933, the [[Texas Legislature]] passed legislation to establish Texas Canyons State Park. Later that year, the park was redesignated Big Bend State Park. In 1935, the [[United States Congress]] passed legislation that would enable the acquisition of the land for a national park.<ref>AN ACT To provide for the establishment of the Big Bend National Park in the State of Texas, and for other purposes. {{USStat|49|393}}, enacted {{date|1935-06-20|mdy}}.</ref> The State of Texas deeded the land that it had acquired to the [[Federal government of the United States|federal government]], and on June 12, 1944, Big Bend National Park became a reality.
