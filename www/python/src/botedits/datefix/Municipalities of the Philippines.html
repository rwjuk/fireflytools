n4A '''municipality''' ({{lang-tl|bayan/munisipalidad}}; {{lang-hil|banwa}}; {{lang-ceb|lungsod/munisipalidad}}; {{lang-pag|baley}}; {{lang-pam|balen/balayan}}; {{lang-bcl|banwaan}}; {{lang-war|bungto}}; {{lang-ilo|ili}}) is a [[local government unit]] (LGU) in the [[Philippines]]. A municipality is called ''town'' in its [[Archaism|archaic]] term: a municipality has the function of a town since its inception. It is distinct from ''city'', which is a different category of local government unit. [[Provinces of the Philippines]] are divided into [[Cities of the Philippines|cities]] and municipalities, which in turn, are divided into [[barangay]]s (formerly [[barrio]]s) – ''villages''. {{as of|2019|9|7|alt=As of {{date|September 7 2019}}}}, there are 1,488 municipalities across the country.{{sfn|PSA|2017}}n4A '''municipality''' ({{lang-tl|bayan/munisipalidad}}; {{lang-hil|banwa}}; {{lang-ceb|lungsod/munisipalidad}}; {{lang-pag|baley}}; {{lang-pam|balen/balayan}}; {{lang-bcl|banwaan}}; {{lang-war|bungto}}; {{lang-ilo|ili}}) is a [[local government unit]] (LGU) in the [[Philippines]].
