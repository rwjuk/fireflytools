107===Implementation (1991–1994)===107===Implementation (1991–1994)===
n108The bill to enact the new Code was introduced into the [[National Assembly of Quebec]] on {{date|1990-12-18}} by [[Gil Rémillard]], who was then Quebec's [[List of Ministers of Justice of Quebec|Minister of Justice]]. It received [[royal assent]] on {{date|1991-12-8}}. It did not come into force until {{date|1994-1-1}}, as the necessary legislation to provide transitional rules determining what matters would be subject to the new Code was not passed until 1992.<ref>''An Act respecting the implementation of the reform of the Civil Code'' (SQ 1992, c. 57)</ref>n108The bill to enact the new Code was introduced into the [[National Assembly of Quebec]] on 18 December 1990 by [[Gil Rémillard]], who was then Quebec's [[List of Ministers of Justice of Quebec|Minister of Justice]]. It received [[royal assent]] on 8 December 1991. It did not come into force until 1 January 1994, as the necessary legislation to provide transitional rules determining what matters would be subject to the new Code was not passed until 1992.<ref>''An Act respecting the implementation of the reform of the Civil Code'' (SQ 1992, c. 57)</ref>
