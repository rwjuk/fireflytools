43'''''Check Your Head''''' is the third studio album by American [[rap rock]] group [[Beastie Boys]], released on April 21, 1992 by [[Capitol Records]]. Three years elapsed between the releases of the band's second studio album ''[[Paul's Boutique]]'' and ''Check Your Head'', which was recorded at the G-Son Studios in [[Atwater Village, Los Angeles|Atwater Village]] in 1991 under the guidance of producer [[Mario Caldato Jr.]], the group's third producer in three albums.
