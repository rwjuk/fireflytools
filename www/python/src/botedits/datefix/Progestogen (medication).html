243Certain progestins can be used at very high doses to [[appetite stimulant|increase appetite]] in conditions like [[cachexia]], [[anorexia (symptom)|anorexia]], and [[wasting syndrome]]s. In general, they are used in combination with certain other steroid medications such as [[dexamethasone]]. Their effects take several weeks to become apparent, but are relatively long-lived when compared to those of [[corticosteroid]]s. Furthermore, they are recognized as being the only medications to increase [[lean body mass]].
