35===Swan River Colony===35===Swan River Colony===
36In August 1826 Fremantle was promoted to captain and in 1828 took command of the 26-gun frigate {{HMS|Challenger|1826|2}},<ref name="loney"/> and sent to claim the west coast of Australia for the United Kingdom. ''Challenger'' was dispatched by the Admiralty from the Cape of Good Hope on 20 March 1829,<ref name="wayb79">{{Cite journal |title=The Western Australian Year Book No. 17, 1979 |journal=Western Australian Year Book |publisher=Australian Bureau of Statistics, Western Australian Office, 1979 |issn=0083-8772}}</ref>{{rp|p11}} anchored in [[Cockburn Sound]] on 27 April and landing on [[Garden Island, Western Australia|Garden Island]] on 2 May. A week later he hoisted the British flag on the south head of the mouth of the [[Swan River (Western Australia)|Swan River]] and took formal possession in the name of His Majesty King [[George IV]] of "all that part of [[New Holland (Australia)]] which is not included within the territory of [[New South Wales]]."<ref name="wayb79"/>{{rp |p11}}<ref>{{Cite book |title=James Stirling: admiral and founding governor of Western Australia |last=Stathem-Drew |first=Pamela |year=2003 |publisher=University of Western Australia Press |location=Crawley, Western Australia 6009 |page=131 |isbn=9781876268947 |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=qy9zAAAAMAAJ}}</ref>36In August 1826 Fremantle was promoted to captain and in 1828 took command of the 26-gun frigate {{HMS|Challenger|1826|2}},<ref name="loney"/> and sent to claim the west coast of Australia for the United Kingdom.
