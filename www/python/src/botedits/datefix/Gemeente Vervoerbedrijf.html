n105Since 1 July 2013, GVB Veren (''veren'' meaning ferries) has been operating ferry services crossing both the [[IJ (Amsterdam)|IJ]] and the [[North Sea Canal]] on behalf of the City of Amsterdam. Most of these ferries offer free rides for pedestrians and cyclists. Within the City of Amsterdam, there are seven ferry routes across the IJ, two of which operate overnight. Outside of Amsterdam, the GVB operates three ferry routes across the North Sea Canal at [[Zaandam]], [[Velsen]] and [[Assendelft]]. The GVB has 19 ferry boats servicing these routes, and is replacing diesel-powered ferries with electrically-operated vessels.
