38'''''Greetings from Asbury Park, N.J.''''' is the debut [[studio album]] by American singer-songwriter [[Bruce Springsteen]]. It was produced by [[Mike Appel]] and Jim Cretecos from June through October 1972 at the budget-priced [[914 Sound Studios]]. The album was released January 5, 1973, by [[Columbia Records]] to average sales but positive critical reviews.38'''''Greetings from Asbury Park, N.J.''''' is the debut [[studio album]] by American singer-songwriter [[Bruce Springsteen]]. It was produced by [[Mike Appel]] and Jim Cretecos from June through October 1972 at the budget-priced [[914 Sound Studios]]. The album was released January 5, 1973, by [[Columbia Records]] to average sales but positive critical reviews.
