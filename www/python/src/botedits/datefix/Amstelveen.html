t110Amstelveen is served by two tram lines running south from [[Amsterdam Zuid station]]. [[Tramlijn 5 (Amsterdam)|Tram line 5]] runs south via Amsterdam Zuid to Stadshart (city centre) in Amstelveen. [[Amsteltram|Tram line 25]] runs between Amsterdam Zuid station and [[Westwijk tram stop|Westwijk]]. Both tram lines serve stops between Amsterdam Zuid and [[Oranjebaan tram stop|Oranjebaan]].
