86== Replacement clients ==86== Replacement clients ==
87An initial project called WinPY attempted to make a replacement open source WinMX client. The WinPY project<ref>{{cite web|url=https://sourceforge.net/projects/winpy/|title=WinPy|website=SourceForge}}</ref> was the most successful replacement client project as a preview alpha version with limited basic functionality was released; however, it quickly stalled due to lack of interest. The WinPY project<ref>{{cite web|url=https://sourceforge.net/projects/winpy/|title=WinPy|website=SourceForge}}</ref> was the most successful replacement client project as a preview alpha version with limited basic functionality was released; however, it quickly stalled due to lack of interest. t88One project announced on a major WinMX community site was initially scheduled to be released in November 2012, however there were many published delays due to lack of skilled programming support.
