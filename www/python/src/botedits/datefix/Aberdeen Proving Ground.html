57===Prompt critical excursion===57===Prompt critical excursion===
58Aberdeen was home to the Army Pulse Radiation Facility Reactor, in 1968. On 6 September 1968, this reactor was the site of a [[Prompt criticality|prompt critical excursion]] during commissioning tests. This accident harmed no personnel but did release enough heat to reach the melting point of the fuel in the core, 1150°C. This caused damage to the fuel components of the reactor, fusing the four central rings together. This is one of thirty-three prompt critical accidents worldwide, between 1949 and 2000.<ref>''[https://web.archive.org/web/*/http://www.csirc.net/docs/reports/la-13638.pdf A Review of Criticality Accidents]'', Los Alamos National Laboratory, LA-13638, May 2000.
