112Also in 1985, the [[Alaska Air Group]] was formed as a [[holding company]] for Alaska Airlines. In 1986 Alaska Air Group acquired regional airline [[Horizon Air]], which remained a separate brand from Alaska Airlines; since then, both airlines have been subsidiaries of Alaska Air Group.
