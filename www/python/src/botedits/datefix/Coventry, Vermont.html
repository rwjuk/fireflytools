109The Phish concert was a two-day event (August 14–15, 2004), held at the [[Northeast Kingdom International Airport|Newport State Airport]]. People began arriving early Friday morning and shortly after the roads became congested. On Friday morning, it had rained. The soft land in the parking area had turned to mud. Cars were then turned away.
