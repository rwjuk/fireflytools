44Six singles were released from the album, with "[[Girls Just Want to Have Fun]]" becoming a worldwide hit and her first song to chart on the ''[[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]]''. "[[Time After Time (Cyndi Lauper song)|Time After Time]]" became her first number-one hit on the chart and experienced similar success worldwide. Lauper found success with the next two singles as well, with both "[[She Bop]]" and "[[All Through the Night (Cyndi Lauper song)|All Through the Night]]" peaking in the top five. t46The album is primarily [[New wave music|new wave]]-based, with many of the songs being influenced by [[synthpop]] and [[pop rock]]. Upon its release, the album received positive reviews from music critics, who noted Lauper's unique vocals. Lauper earned several awards and accolades for the album, including two [[Grammy Awards]] at the [[27th Grammy Awards]], one of which was for [[Grammy Award for Best New Artist|Best New Artist]]. ''She's So Unusual'' peaked at number four on the [[Billboard 200|''Billboard'' 200]] chart and stayed in the chart's top forty for 65 weeks. It has sold over 6 million copies in the United States and 16 million copies worldwide.
