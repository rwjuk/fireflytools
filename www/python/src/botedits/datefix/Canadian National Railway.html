83Canadian National Railways was born out of both wartime and domestic urgency. Until the rise of the personal automobile and creation of taxpayer-funded all-weather highways, railways were the only viable long-distance land transportation available in Canada. As such, their operation consumed a great deal of public and political attention. Canada was one of many nations to engage in [[railway nationalization]] in order to safeguard critical transportation infrastructure during the [[First World War]].83Canadian National Railways was born out of both wartime and domestic urgency.
