36'''''The B-52's''''' is the debut album by the [[Athens, Georgia]]-based [[new wave music|new wave]] band [[the B-52's]]. The [[kitsch]]y lyrics and mood, and the hook-laden harmonies helped establish a fanbase for the band, who went on to release several chart-topping singles. The album cover was designed by [[Tony Wright (artist)|Tony Wright]] (credited as ''Sue Ab Surd'').36'''''The B-52's''''' is the debut album by the [[Athens, Georgia]]-based [[new wave music|new wave]] band [[the B-52's]]. The [[kitsch]]y lyrics and mood, and the hook-laden harmonies helped establish a fanbase for the band, who went on to release several chart-topping singles.
