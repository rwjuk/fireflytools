382*10 November 1990: Thai Airways International Flight 306, an [[Airbus A300|Airbus A300-600]] flying from Yangon to Bangkok was hijacked by four Burmese students demanding to be taken to India. 386*3 March 2001: [[Thai Airways International Flight 114]], a [[Boeing 737 Classic|Boeing 737-400]] (HS-TDC, ''[[Narathiwat]]''), bound for [[Chiang Mai]] from Bangkok, was destroyed by an explosion of the center wing tank resulting from ignition of the flammable fuel-air mixture in the tank while the aircraft was parked at the gate in Bangkok. The source of the ignition energy for the explosion could not be determined with certainty, but the most likely source was an explosion originating at the center wing tank pump as a result of running the pump in the presence of metal shavings and a fuel-air mixture. One crew member died.<ref>{{ASN accident|title=HS-TDC|id=20010303-1 |access-date= 14 June 2009}}</ref>386*3 March 2001: [[Thai Airways International Flight 114]], a [[Boeing 737 Classic|Boeing 737-400]] (HS-TDC, ''[[Narathiwat]]''), bound for [[Chiang Mai]] from Bangkok, was destroyed by an explosion of the center wing tank resulting from ignition of the flammable fuel-air mixture in the tank while the aircraft was parked at the gate in Bangkok.
